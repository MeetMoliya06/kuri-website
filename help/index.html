Frequently Asked Questions

How is the algorithm score calculated?

Kuri computes restaurant scores dynamically using a combination of your Food, Ambiance, and Presentation ratings. Our system also weights reviews based on trust factors to prevent manipulation.

Can I add a restaurant that isn't listed?

Currently, Kuri focuses on verified restaurants in Surat. We are continuously adding new venues. If you believe a major spot is missing, feel free to reach out to us!

How do Pioneer Rewards work?

If you are among the first 5 users to review a newly listed restaurant on Kuri, you will receive bonus XP as a Pioneer reward, helping you level up faster.

Still need help?

Our support team is here for you. Email us at hello@kuri.blog and we'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.
